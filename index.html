Our Mission

Today's mobile duopoly controls what apps ship, who gets paid, and what rules apply. Solana Mobile is building the alternative — an open mobile platform.

SKR is the native asset of the Solana Mobile ecosystem. It distributes control, powers curation, and aligns incentives across builders, users, and hardware partners.

Via SKR, the community governs, builders keep what they earn, and users own a stake in the value they create.

Decentralized Governance: Stake to Guardians who verify devices and curate the dApp Store.

Ecosystem Ownership: Rewards, community treasury funds, growth initiatives, and protocol development.

Aligned Incentives: Builders, users, and hardware partners share in the value they create.

Inflation Schedule
